Courses

Team-based practical training provides an opportunity to put information networking into practice. By making the most of the specialized knowledge and skills they acquire in each course, students in a team spanning the four courses receive simulated startup training.

Engineering

Students acquire the skill to support networking technically and implement new information services.

Design

Students acquire the skill to support networking from the viewpoint of designing and giving shape to new information services and products.

Business

Developing managers who can start new businesses through networking

Civil System

Acquiring the skill to build application of ICT to create enriched society and well-being with networking

The Campus

The campus is equipped with Wi-Fi, and a sufficient bandwidth of Internet connectivity has been allocated to support information technology education, which enables students to bring their PCs and smartphones to use for classes and research.

The Lockers

Personal locker allows students to put personal items, but the key is API control, and it is practical to write an application that uses it-the locker is also an IoT teaching material.

The Media Center

The Media Center located in a public area on the first floor of INIAD is a space which focuses on facilitating face-to-face communication by providing media functions that cannot be implemented over the Net.

The INIAD Hall

A hall that can be used for lectures and symposiums.

Exhibition Area

The space is equipped with exhibition furniture such as stands and large screen projectors to support panel, full-scale real demonstrations, and projector exhibitions.

INIAD Makers' Hub

Makers' Hub with equipment such as 3D printer and laser cutter for rapid prototyping.

Open for registration

The Faculty of Information Networking for Innovation and Design holds an entrance examination to evaluate various types of skills possessed by applicants. Please apply for the entrance examination which is best suited to you.
